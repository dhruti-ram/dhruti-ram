## 📊 Tableau Portfolio

### 1. 🧑‍💼 HR Attrition & Workforce Analytics Dashboard
> Analyzed IBM HR dataset (1,470 employees) to identify attrition drivers and retention risks.

**Key Insights:**
- Employees in Sales with < 2 years tenure are 3x more likely to leave — indicates onboarding gap
- Overtime workers show 30%+ higher attrition — burnout is the primary flight risk signal
